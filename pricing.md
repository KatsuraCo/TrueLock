# TrueLock Pricing

Last updated: 2026-04-08

TrueLock is an offline-first software application for protected file and message delivery.

## Public pricing summary

- Opening and verifying capsules is always free for recipients.
- Pro for creators has two one-time checkout options.
- Business pilots, white-label work, and licensing are custom-scoped.

## Plans

| Plan | For | Price | Billing | Includes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | Recipients and basic evaluation | $0 | No subscription | Open and verify capsules, basic creation flow with limits, basic sharing/import flow, no account required |
| Pro | Active creators | $10 crypto / $20 card | One-time payment | Everything in Free, advanced creator capabilities, extended policy configuration, scenario templates, custom template workflows, batch-oriented workflows; 1 device on crypto checkout or up to 2 devices on MoonPay card checkout |
| Business pilot | Teams validating one workflow | Custom quote | Scoped engagement | One use case, one workflow, one stakeholder group, short validation period, direct evaluation support |
| White-label / SDK / format licensing | Partners and product teams | Custom quote | Scoped engagement | Integration discussion, packaging, commercial rights, deployment model, support expectations |

## Checkout options

- Card checkout via MoonPay: $20, 2 Pro licenses, up to 2 devices total.
- Hosted crypto checkout: $10, 1 Pro license, 1 device.
- The buyer must provide an email so the Pro key can be sent after payment verification.

## Release scope

- Public release scope today: Windows + Android.
- iOS, macOS, and Linux are not public production platforms today.

## Security messaging boundary

- Do not treat TrueLock as magic DRM.
- Access enforcement is application-layer.
- A fully compromised client or runtime can bypass local controls.
- Public trust documents are available on the site for review.
