# Privacy Policy - TrueLock

**Last Updated: March 5, 2026**
**Effective Date: March 5, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

TrueLock ("we," "us," "our," or "Application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect
TrueLock is designed with privacy as a core principle:
- We DO NOT collect your encrypted data.
- We DO NOT access your passwords or encryption keys.
- We DO NOT track your personal files.
- We DO NOT store your files in the cloud.
- We DO NOT collect analytics or telemetry.
- We DO NOT sell your data.

### 2.2 Information Collected
We do not collect personal information, analytics, or telemetry.

## 3. How We Use Information

We do not collect user data, so there is nothing to process or monetize.

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Local-First Architecture
- All encryption/decryption happens on your device.
- No data leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.
- The Application works offline for core functionality.
- **Policy verification:** If you use time/geo policies, the app may contact public time or map services to reduce tampering. No files or keys are transmitted.

### 4.2 Encryption Standards
- AES-256-GCM for file/capsule encryption.
- Argon2id-based key derivation in current password flows.
- Additional authenticated crypto paths may use ChaCha20-Poly1305 where applicable.

### 4.3 Security Boundary
- Access enforcement is implemented at application layer.
- If a device/runtime is fully compromised, local controls may be bypassed.
- Weak passwords can be brute-forced offline if an attacker obtains encrypted files.

## 5. Third-Party Services

TrueLock does not send your files or keys to any server. If you enable time or geo policies, the app may contact:
- Public time servers (time verification)
- Map/geocoding services (address search or map tiles)

These requests never include your encrypted files or passwords.

## 6. Data Retention

- Your encrypted files are stored only on your device.
- We do not store copies of your data.
- If you delete the Application, local data is removed from that device.

## 7. Children's Privacy

TrueLock is designed for users of all ages.

Because TrueLock does not collect personal data, analytics, or telemetry, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If local law requires parental or guardian consent for software use by minors, such consent must be provided by the parent or legal guardian.

## 8. Your Rights & Choices

- You can use the app offline.
- You can avoid optional network checks by not using time/geo policies.
- You control your local data.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
