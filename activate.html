TrueLock Pro activation

Activate TrueLock Pro.

To receive a TrueLock Pro license, open the request form below and send your email and TrueLock device ID. TrueLock Pro costs $10. After we receive your request, we prepare an EasyStaff invoice and send a secure payment link to the email address from the form.

How payment and activation work

  1. Open the license request form.
  2. Enter your email and TrueLock device ID from the app.
  3. If you paid by crypto, enter your USDT Transaction ID in the form.
  4. Send the form.
  5. We create an EasyStaff invoice and send the secure payment link to the email address from the form.
  6. After payment is confirmed, we send your signed offline TrueLock Pro license code to the email address from the form.
